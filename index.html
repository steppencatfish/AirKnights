AirKnights

Discover the Heroes of the Sky

Our museum, located in the charming city of Chicago, is dedicated to preserving the legacy of fighter aviation during World War II. Discover the courage, innovation, and heroism that defined an era. Step into history and explore the remarkable stories of WWII fighter pilots who displayed unmatched courage and shaped history in the skies. Learn about their missions, their aircraft, and the legacy they left in the skies.

Meet our team

Behind Airknights is a passionate team of historians, engineers, and volunteers who work tirelessly to restore and preserve iconic aircraft. Together, we bring history to life for future generations.

About AirKnights

Located in Chicago, the AirKnights Museum is dedicated to preserving the history of fighter aviation and honoring the heroes of the skies.
